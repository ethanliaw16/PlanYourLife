What is Plan Your Life?

This is an app that helps users plan their life! Insert a query and we can add it to your Google Calendar or Google Tasks. This streamlines Google application usage.

Can I use this without a Google account?

Unfortunately, no.

How do I put in a query?

Acceptable queries follow the below pattern:

  • Add <task name> - adds to Tasks
  • Add event <event name> on <mm/dd/yy> - adds to Calendar
